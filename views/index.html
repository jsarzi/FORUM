PARIS

The wonderful capital of France. The city of LOVE. The city is known for its architecture and also for its gastronomy. You will find amazing restaurants, from local bistros to restaurants with michelin starts. Come visit this super city for a week end or a week.

MADRID

Madrid, the Spanish capital. A small tip, 'tio' means 'bro' in Spain. Be ready to eat late and listen to music evrytime you will walk around the city. Madrid is really dynamic and you will maybe have some times to take salsa clases.

BERLIN

Brelin, the German capital. Take some german clases because it is not an easy language, but don't worry, the german will in general understand you if you speak english... not like the french.If you were looking to party, you choose the right destination! Berlin is also the capital of ELECTRONIC MUSICS. And if you wanted chill days, Berlin will ofers you a lot of different museums to know more about history.
